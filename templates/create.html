How it works

  1. Fill out this form to set up your event.

  2. On the next page, create some questions to ask your attendees during the event.

  3. Get your attendees involved!

    If people are tweeting, make sure they use your hashtag, and tell them about your event's page on re:chattr.

  4. Once your event starts, re:chattr will monitor Twitter for posts mentioning @${user.username} or your hashtag.

    It will also send out your questions and collect people's answers.

  5. After the event is over, browse through the comments posted on Twitter and see the answers to your questions.

    Respond to your feedback and participate in discussions with your attendees.
