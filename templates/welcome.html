Manage your backchannel

People may already be talking about your event on Twitter. re:chattr collects that discussion and saves it in one place.

Targetted feedback

Ever wonder what your audience thought about a specific slide or idea in your presentation? Use re:chattr's feedback polls to ask your audience simple multiple-choice questions.

Here's how it works:

  1. Sign in using Twitter to set up a new event.
  2. Come up with a special #hashtag for your event and set up a few questions for your audience.
  3. Give your presentation! (remember to tell your audience to tweet using your #hashtag)
  4. Visit re:chattr for the results.
